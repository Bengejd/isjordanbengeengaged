How did Jordan propose?

Erica and I love to do Escape Rooms, but we haven't had the opportunity to do one since moving to Omaha, two years ago. So I thought it would be the perfect cover.

Once I had the general idea worked out, I reached out to one of her friends and asked if her and her husband would be willing to help me with the proposal. She enthusiastically agreed and covertly had Erica get her nails done with her, under the guise of her going on vacation the following week.

After a lot of research, I decided to call Get Out Omaha and ask if they would be willing to facilitate a proposal; to which they were more than happy to oblige.

After discussing my ideas with the host, I decided the best option was for the final clue to be the proposal. I had an old scroll printed with the words "Will You Marry Me" on it. Making sure to relay to the friend & her husband, that Erica was to be the one who opened the drawer with the scroll in it, and to let her open the scroll.
